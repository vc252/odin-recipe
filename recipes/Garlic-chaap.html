Ingredients

  • 150 gms Soya Nuggets
  • 3 medium size onions
  • 4 medium-size green capsicum
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1/2 tbsp soya sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1/2 tbsp ginger paste
  • 1/2 tbsp garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp chilly powder
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 tbsp water
  • Oil to fry

Steps

  1. Boil the soya nuggets till they become soft.
  2. Drain all the water and keep the nuggets aside.
  3. Take one bowl for preparing nuggets marination.
  4. Put water & ginger pastes, maida, edible food colour, red chilly powder in a bowl and mix well. Add salt to taste and mix.
  5. Put all the nuggets in the same bowl, mix well and leave it for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Put oil in the pan for frying nuggets. Fry them on low flame till nuggets change into red colour.
  7. Put little oil in the pan, saute finely sliced onions and capsicum till its color changes to golden.
  8. Add all the fried nuggets and mix it well adding some salt, red chilly powder to taste.
  9. Mix soya sauce, vinegar and tomato ketchup properly and mix it to the nuggets and leave pan covered for 5 minutes on low flame.
  10. Garnish it with coriander leaves and serve it hot.
